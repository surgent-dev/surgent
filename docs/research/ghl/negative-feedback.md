## 1. EMAIL DELIVERABILITY DISASTER

**Severity: CRITICAL** | Most commonly cited complaint across ALL sources

GHL uses Mailgun (branded as "LC Email") for email sending. Users migrating from dedicated platforms report catastrophic drops in deliverability.

**Specific complaints:**

- One agency owner reported open rates collapsing from **35-40% on Keap to a pathetic 9%** on GHL -- a 50%+ decline with identical audience and content
- Shared IP addresses mean other users' spam behavior can tank YOUR deliverability even if your practices are clean
- **No built-in email throttling** -- emails are blasted out all at once, overwhelming SMTP servers and triggering spam filters
- GHL's link tracking modifies original URLs, which gets flagged as suspicious by spam filters
- Users report around **30% lower delivery rate** compared to previous email platforms
- One user on the GHL Ideas portal filed an "URGENT" request noting GHL's lack of throttling is "breaking deliverability for users with custom SMTP"

**User quote:** "My email engagement dropped dramatically after migrating to GoHighLevel, even though my audience and content remained the same."

**Impact:** Email-first marketers consistently leave GHL for dedicated tools like ActiveCampaign or Mailchimp.

## 2. CUSTOMER SUPPORT DECLINE

**Severity: CRITICAL** | Second most common complaint

Support has degraded significantly over time according to users across all review platforms.

**Specific complaints:**

- **"Support went from horrific, to good once you can talk to someone then to, terrible."**
- **"Chat doesn't help they seem to just want to create a ticket and then it takes a week or more"**
- **"whoever picks up the ticket DOES NOT watch the looms or the info provided"**
- **"there is no way to chat with an agent anymore. they make you zoom call which i would prefer not to do"**
- **"Support has been horrific. They used to have fantastic support that really knew what they were doing"**
- **"I have not had a single positive experience in the past few months"**
- Tier 1 support described as confused, poorly trained, unable to handle basic inquiries
- Support agents reassigned daily, requiring customers to re-explain everything
- Response times stretch to 24-48 hours; complex issues get circular responses
- Priority support costs an additional **$300/month**
- One BBB complaint documented **70+ support tickets** with no resolution and **$4,400+ in claimed damages**
- Training materials described as "often out of date, wrong, or non-existent"

## 3. HIDDEN COSTS & DECEPTIVE PRICING

**Severity: HIGH**

The headline pricing ($97-$497/mo) dramatically understates actual costs.

**Specific hidden costs:**

- Email sending: charged per email through LC Email/Mailgun
- SMS: charged per message via Twilio
- Phone calls: charged per minute
- AI features: credit-based pricing
- WhatsApp: subscription + per-conversation charges
- HIPAA compliance: **+$297/month permanently** once activated
- WordPress hosting: additional monthly fee
- Realistic add-on costs for a typical agency: **$70-$150/month** on top of subscription

**User quotes:**

- "Nickle-and-dimed with sporadic fees for core functionality"
- "Low sticker price was tempting, but just not realistic in practice"
- "Getting charged every time they send an email through GoHighLevel's servers, which for high volume users adds up to hundreds of dollars"

**Impact:** Total monthly costs for agencies regularly exceed **$500-$700+**, far beyond the advertised $97-$497.

## 4. PLATFORM BUGS & STABILITY ISSUES

**Severity: HIGH**

Rapid feature releases have introduced persistent instability.

**Specific bugs reported:**

- **171 completely irrelevant emails sent to users who should NOT have received them** -- happened 3 days in a row, different batch each day
- Automations sending emails to wrong recipients despite careful setup
- Workflows firing incorrectly -- duplicate emails, wrong contacts, triggers not activating
- Website editor save/publish function failing; work disappears overnight
- Social planner broken for extended periods (one BBB complaint cited it broken since Nov 20)
- CSS and margin controls non-functional in builder
- Forms on client pages failing to load during outages

**Stability data:**

- **124+ outages tracked over ~2 years** (StatusGator)
- **20 incidents in last 90 days** with median duration of 38 minutes
- Users report "downtime every month"
- Status page itself doesn't load during outages

**User quotes:**

- **"The CRM clearly has great features, but it is SUPER buggy. Things constantly not loading properly is a big problem."**
- **"I can't resell this buggy thing to customers it will ruin my brand"**
- **"Whole site randomly becomes unclickable like ~50% of the time I load a page"**
- **"The GHL Platform is painfully slow...it's too expensive for us to use this if it has our customers churning"**
- **"Giving demos to clients or simply establishing trust and quality in the first few weeks of a client using the system as SaaS is difficult when they're confused as to why nothing works without glitches."**

## 5. SLOW PERFORMANCE & PAGE SPEED

**Severity: HIGH**

Both the backend dashboard and published pages/funnels suffer from poor performance.

**Backend dashboard:**

- "The backend dashboard in GoHighLevel was a little slower than what I am accustomed to" with multi-second delays when loading tools
- "Many users are experiencing slow loading times that impact their productivity"
- Load times described as "AWFUL still"
- Performance particularly bad for users in certain geographic locations (Brazil, etc.)

**Published pages/funnels:**

- GHL page load times average **4-5 seconds** vs. ClickFunnels' **~1.2 seconds**
- Google PageSpeed Insights flags Lead Connector and HighLevel assets as main speed killers
- Mobile performance especially poor -- built desktop-first, adapted for mobile as afterthought
- Slow speeds directly translate to lower conversion rates

**User quote:** "Slow speeds can result in losing sales when showing the platform to new clients"

## 6. STEEP LEARNING CURVE & POOR ONBOARDING

**Severity: HIGH**

**Specific complaints:**

- Full operational setup requires **2-4 weeks**
- Users describe finding themselves "in training sessions multiple times per week for hours"
- GHL does NOT provide client onboarding -- only onboarding for agency account owners, not their clients
- Multiple third-party companies (Extendly, Growthable, Agency Support) exist solely to fill the onboarding gap
- Training documentation frequently out of date or non-existent
- Complex webhooks and integrations confusing for beginners
- "Too much of a slog for anyone who wants to be productive in less than a day"

**Impact:** Created an entire cottage industry of third-party GHL onboarding services, indicating the platform itself fails at teaching users.

## 7. CLUNKY, UNINTUITIVE UI/UX

**Severity: HIGH**

**Specific complaints:**

- Dashboard described as "quite simple and nothing fancy" -- looks "like a WordPress dashboard"
- Interface has "glaring blemishes from a design perspective"
- Settings "unexplainably scattered in totally different sections"
- Funnel builder has **3 separate settings panels** with confusing navigation
- Contact tab feels "squished" on smaller screens
- Platform "tried to jam too much stuff" into one interface
- Simple tasks like editing workflows involve "digging through menu systems"
- Feels like "a work-in-progress rather than a polished product"
- Smart lists become "bloated and slow" with too many contacts/tags, appearing "messy and disorganized"

**User quote:** "The interface isn't intuitive. Settings are scattered everywhere, and simple tasks like editing funnels need multiple confusing steps."

## 9. JACK OF ALL TRADES, MASTER OF NONE

**Severity: HIGH** | Fundamental architectural complaint

Every individual tool within GHL is inferior to its dedicated competitor.

**Specific comparisons:**

- **Funnel builder**: Not as refined as ClickFunnels
- **CRM**: Not as intuitive as Pipedrive or HubSpot
- **Email editor**: Basic compared to ActiveCampaign
- **Email deliverability**: Far worse than Mailchimp/ActiveCampaign
- **Course builder**: Dated compared to Kajabi or Teachable
- **Website builder**: Like "Wix and website builders from the 2010s"
- **Reporting**: "Basic compared to HubSpot" -- lacks advanced attribution, custom report builders, executive dashboards
- **AI features**: "Slaps on AI features for show" that "don't genuinely save time"

**User quote:** "HighLevel is like a jack of all trades but not a master of one. They do everything alright but issues always come up because nothing is perfect."

## 10. WEBSITE/FUNNEL BUILDER LIMITATIONS

**Severity: MEDIUM-HIGH**

**Specific complaints:**

- UX described as "a little like Wix and website builders from the 2010s"
- Modules and functions are "quite limited" -- unsuitable for intermediate to advanced developers
- Embedded forms don't render properly on external sites, requiring extensive CSS adjustments
- Page load speeds 3-4x slower than ClickFunnels
- Generic templates that "haven't been proven in real, revenue-generating businesses"
- Order bumps, cart management, and upsell sequences less polished than competitors
- Limited customization -- agencies "can't create truly branded client experiences"
- $10/month hosting package considered poor value

## 11. WEAK REPORTING & ANALYTICS

**Severity: MEDIUM-HIGH**

**Specific complaints:**

- "Basic compared to HubSpot" -- lacks advanced attribution modeling, custom report builders, executive dashboards
- Users with Google Workspace or third-party email providers cannot access accurate campaign statistics (delivered, bounced, spam metrics)
- Full reporting only available to LC Email/Mailgun users
- Limited ability to create custom reports across different objects with custom fields
- Clients paying for external tools like Plecto because GHL reporting is insufficient
- No indication in UI that missing data is a provider limitation vs. system error

## 12. API & INTEGRATION LIMITATIONS

**Severity: MEDIUM-HIGH**

**Specific complaints:**

- Developer described **"API v2 is absolute garbage"**
- OAuth and marketplace approval required even for simple one-off integrations
- 10-day wait for marketplace approval frustrates developers
- Rate limit: max 100 API requests per 10 seconds per app
- Advanced API access locked behind Agency Pro plan
- GHL support explicitly does NOT provide API development or debugging assistance
- Integrations described as "barely go beyond the basics and are unreliable"
- Many integrations require Zapier, custom scripts, or external APIs
- Only supports basic services like Stripe, Facebook, Shopify

## 13. BILLING & CANCELLATION NIGHTMARES

**Severity: HIGH** | 22 BBB complaints in 3 years

**Specific BBB cases:**

- User charged **$9,930.14** for unauthorized yearly subscriptions after requesting cancellation (eventually refunded $13,380.13 after dispute)
- User charged $97 twice during trial despite documented cancellation attempts
- Users report "you can't simply cancel the trial by yourself -- you have to reach out to them via email"
- Account suspended without notice while maintaining billing
- Continued charges after cancellation described as "systematic fraud"
- Only 3 of 22 BBB complaints marked as "Resolved" (rest only "Answered")

**Company response pattern:**

- Blames customers for "system complications"
- Cites Terms of Service as absolute defense
- Questions evidence authenticity
- Offers partial settlements as "goodwill"

## 18. A2P 10DLC / SMS REGISTRATION NIGHTMARE

**Severity: MEDIUM**

**Specific complaints:**

- Complex multi-step registration process required just to send SMS
- "Even tiny mistakes can lead to rejection"
- Brand rejection for EIN mismatch, invalid website, or PO Box usage
- Manual vetting process with $15.75 fee per campaign
- Requires patience, specialized knowledge, and perfect documentation
- Process is industry-wide but GHL doesn't simplify it

- Prevents migration to competing platform
- Customer described as "domain hostage situation"
- When you build on GHL infrastructure, migrating away is extremely difficult
