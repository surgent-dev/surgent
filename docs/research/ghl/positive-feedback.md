## KNOWN WEAKNESSES (For Context)

While the focus of this research is positive feedback, these are the most commonly mentioned drawbacks that users accept as trade-offs:

1. **Steep learning curve** - Takes weeks to master; interface can feel overwhelming
2. **Email deliverability** - Relies on Mailgun; weaker than dedicated email tools
3. **Individual features not best-in-class** - Jack of all trades, master of none (each feature is 70-80% as good as dedicated competitors)
4. **Hidden usage costs** - SMS, email, phone, AI usage fees on top of subscription
5. **Mobile app historically weak** - Significantly improved with v4.0 redesign but still catching up
6. **UI/UX complexity** - Dense interface, especially for non-technical users
